Throwback to 8th grade: February 2019

For a class project, I needed to teach myself how to put together a robot which could do certain tasks (pick up ping pong balls and move them to a pedestal). At first this was very challenging because I had no prior experience and no assistance towards designing/wiring the robot. After researching, I really enjoyed the project. This project allowed me to have more interest into the STEM field and I love how my teacher made the class do it independently with trial and error.
