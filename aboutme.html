I really love architecture and modeling different structures.

Based off my interests, I decided that I want my profession to be in the medical/engineering field. I realized that I could combine physics, computer science, and engineering all within one specific major. Biomedical Engineering. Biomed supports the ideas of artifical intelligence, the engineering of a prosthetics, and health for those around us. It involves many things that I enjoy and its a profession that I find passion in.
